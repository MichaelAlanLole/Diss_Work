Air Quality Around The Globe

Welcome to my interactive experience on air pollution, with a focus on vehicle emissions. This includes Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂), Particulate Matter (PM2.5 and PM10), and Carbon Monoxide (CO), all of which are released into the air we breathe by the vehicles we drive, significantly impacting urban environments.

Please navigate around the globe and explore how these pollutants are produced, and how they effect the quality of the air we breathe!

Please click on any continent to view the air quality of its major cities!
