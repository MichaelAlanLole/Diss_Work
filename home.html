Air Quality Around The Globe

Welcome to my interactive experience on air pollution, with a focus on vehicle emissions, which significantly impact urban environments and the air we breathe.

Use your mouse to click and drag the globe, and click the buttons above it to explore the emissions produced!

Please click on any red marker above a continent to view the air quality of its major cities!
